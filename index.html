Free Palestine

Historical Timeline

1948

Al-Nakba (The Catastrophe)

Over 750,000 Palestinians were forcibly displaced following the creation of Israel, leading to mass exile and loss of Palestinian land.

1967

Six-Day War & Occupation

Israel occupied the West Bank, Gaza, and East Jerusalem, leading to military rule over Palestinians and the beginning of illegal settlements.

1987

First Intifada

A large-scale Palestinian uprising against Israeli occupation, marked by civil disobedience and clashes, leading to global awareness of the Palestinian struggle.

2008

Gaza War

Israel launched a major military operation in Gaza, causing extensive casualties and destruction, worsening the humanitarian crisis.

2012

Palestine Recognized by the UN

The United Nations upgraded Palestine to a "non-member observer state" status, recognizing its sovereignty on the international stage.
